Before You Write In

These steps resolve the most common issues quickly:

  • Force-quit the app and relaunch it — this fixes most unexpected behavior.
  • Make sure you're running the latest version of Big Deal Bridge from the App Store.
  • To restore a subscription on a new device, tap Restore Purchases on the subscription screen while signed into your original Apple ID.
  • For billing questions, go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions — Apple handles all transactions directly.

When Emailing Support

The more detail you include, the faster we can help. Please mention:

  • A description of what happened and what you expected to happen
  • Any error messages you saw
  • If relevant, the name of the tournament or PBN file you were using
  • Your device type (iPhone or iPad)
🕐

We typically respond within one to two business days. For subscription billing issues, Apple Support can often resolve things faster than we can.

App Store & Subscription Management

All purchases and subscriptions are processed directly by Apple. To manage or cancel a subscription, visit Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions on your device, or use the link below.
