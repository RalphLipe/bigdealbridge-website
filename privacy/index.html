Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect limited, anonymized usage data to improve our app and understand how users interact with our subscription services. This includes:

Analytics Data

  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Subscription-related events (purchases, renewals, cancellations, and restoration attempts)
  • Device and platform information (iOS version, device type)
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • General location information (country/region level only)

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Personal identifying information (name, email, phone number)
  • Precise location data
  • Contact lists or personal files
  • Game results or player names
  • Any images from your camera

Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect and analyze usage data. Firebase may collect additional technical information such as:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID, which can be reset by users)
  • IP addresses (used for general location and immediately discarded)
  • App performance and crash data

Data Usage

The anonymized data we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Understand subscription conversion and retention patterns
  • Fix bugs and performance issues
  • Make informed decisions about new features

Data Retention

Analytics data is automatically deleted after 14 months. Users can opt out of analytics collection through their device settings by limiting ad tracking.

Your Privacy Rights

  • Analytics data cannot be tied back to individual users
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any collected data with third parties for marketing purposes

For more information about Google Firebase's privacy practices, please review Google's Privacy Policy.
