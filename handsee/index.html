After the hand, see every player's cards and high card point count exactly as recorded in the PBN file — the complete hand on one screen.

Lesson Hands for Teachers

Prepare lesson hands as PBN files. Students deal them blind and play the hand — then reveal the record and your notes for a guided teaching moment.

How It Works

Deal, Play, Reveal

1

Choose a PBN Hand

Load a lesson hand from a PBN file — your own, a teacher's, or one from a library.

2

Deal It Blind

Hold each card face-down over the selfie camera. The app tells you exactly which player gets each card — the distribution stays hidden until you're ready.

3

Play, Then Reveal

Bid and play the hand normally. Afterwards, reveal the full hand record — every card, every player's HCP, and everything else the PBN contains.
