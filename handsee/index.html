What Handsee Does

📚

Built-in Lesson Library

Browse a curated library of lesson hands, each designed to illustrate a specific bridge concept. New curriculum is added to the library regularly.

🃏

Blind Dealing

Hold each card face-down over the selfie camera. The app directs every card to the right player — no one sees the distribution until it's time to review.

📋

Lesson Review

After the hand is played, reveal the full lesson: every player's cards, high card points, the recorded auction, and any teaching notes included with the hand.

🎓

Great for the Classroom

Teachers can walk students through hands from the lesson library — deal them blind, play them, then open the review and discuss what happened together.

🆓

Free to Use

Free to download and use — no sign-up required. The full lesson library is available at no cost. Additional content may be offered in the future.

📱

iPhone & iPad Native

Built specifically for iOS. Works beautifully on any iPhone or iPad — no internet connection required once the app is installed.

How It Works

Deal, Play, Review

1

Select a Lesson

Browse the built-in library and choose a hand. Each lesson is designed around a specific bridge concept.

2

Deal It Blind

Hold each card face-down over the selfie camera. The app tells you exactly which player gets each card — the distribution stays hidden until you review.

3

Play, Then Review

Bid and play the hand normally. Then reveal the lesson — the full hand record, the auction, and the teaching notes.
